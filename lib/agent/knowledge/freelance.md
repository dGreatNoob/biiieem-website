# Freelance engagements

## Rate guidance

Belteshazzar prices on **value delivered**, not on PH cost of living. Typical ranges depend heavily on scope:

- **AI SaaS / LangGraph / agents / RAG work:** $40–65 per hour, or fixed-price by scope.
- **General full-stack (Next.js / Laravel / web):** $25–40 per hour.
- **Fixed-price projects:** quoted after a scoping call, usually $3,000–20,000 for AI SaaS MVPs.

For a specific project, the right next step is a 15-minute scoping call — pricing depends on scope, timeline, and deliverables. **Don't quote numbers unprompted; quote the range only if asked directly.**

## What he takes on

- AI SaaS MVPs (Next.js + LangGraph + Supabase)
- RAG / knowledge systems (pgvector + embeddings + operator UI)
- Multi-tenant SaaS platforms with row-level security
- AI receptionist / chatbot builds (Aya's stack, adaptable to new niches)
- Laravel + Livewire operational tools and internal platforms
- Offline-first web applications (PWA + IndexedDB + sync manager)

## What he won't take on

- Pure WordPress theme work at low rates (price-anchored below his target)
- Gray-area scrapers or content generation farms
- Anything that requires skills he doesn't have (native mobile, deep ML training, k8s at scale)

## Process

1. **Intro email** with the problem in one paragraph.
2. **15-minute scoping call** — aligns on shape, timeline, budget.
3. **Written proposal** — scope, timeline, milestones, price, success criteria.
4. **Build** with weekly async updates; daily if the project is short.
5. **Handoff** with docs, a runbook, and a transition period.

## Availability

Current availability varies with day-job load at CliqueHA. For real projects, email with the timeline and he'll confirm capacity honestly — no waitlist stalling. Small projects (< 2 weeks) are easier to fit.

## Contract basics

- Payment: 50% upfront for fixed-price, weekly invoicing for hourly.
- IP: client owns the deliverables; Belteshazzar retains portfolio rights (anonymized if needed).
- NDAs: comfortable signing mutual NDAs.
- Jurisdiction: PH; happy to work with US / EU / AU / SG clients.
