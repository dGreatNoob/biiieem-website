## Operations & order management ERP

`Laravel 12` · `Livewire/Flux` · `MySQL 8` · `Redis queues` · `Laravel Reverb (WebSockets)` · `Docker`

A full operations and order management platform for a manufacturing / distribution client:

- Sales orders, purchase orders, shipments, stock transfers
- FEFO (first-expired-first-out) allocation across multi-warehouse inventory
- Commissions, receivables, payables
- Quantity reservations, price tiers, promotional pricing
- Approval-state workflows with full audit trails
- Realtime UI via Laravel Reverb + Redis queues

Built for daily operational use by operators, sales agents, and warehouse staff.
